## Bottom line (as of 1 July 2026)

The internal-dynamics / hidden-state / world-model axis "D" (emphatically *not* depth) has become the organizing frontier of VLA research: the strongest 2026 policies win by carrying a *latent world model* as their D, and a parallel interpretability literature has shown that VLA hidden states contain probeable, steerable, value-like structure [2][3][10][11][13][14][15]. prisoma is well-positioned in this landscape as a *diagnostic* layer — it treats D as a first-class, source-agnostic input and asks an information-theoretic question (what does D uniquely/redundantly/synergistically tell us about the action, and does D predict physically-valid 3D trajectories) that the field is only beginning to ask with PID [17][18]. ## 1. What "D" means — and why it is not depth

In the prisoma VLDA contract, the four axes are V = pre-fusion vision representation, L = text/instruction representation, A = action output (e.g. d=7), and **D = the Dynamics / hidden-state / world-model / internal-state axis** (repo-internal). D is explicitly *not* a depth map or any geometric range channel; the naming collision with "depth" is a persistent hazard the contract calls out. D is instead "the internal simulation": the policy's own evolving belief about how the world will move. The contract enumerates three concrete forms (repo-internal): `D_explicit` (deliberately exposed world-knowledge channels), `D_hidden[k]` (a *selected* policy-backbone hidden-state layer, where the layer choice is an experimental variable chosen by a layerwise physics-probe procedure), and `D_fused` (post-fusion state).

This is exactly the quantity the 2026 literature has converged on. A recent survey defines a **World Action Model (WAM)** as one in which a predicted future observation helps *produce, choose, or check* the action — distinct from vanilla VLAs that regress actions directly with no explicit future modeling [5]. That "predicted future observation," whether in pixel space, latent space, or video-generation-free form, *is* D. The critical prisoma test on D — does it predict physically-valid 3D trajectories — is a world-model test in exactly this WAM sense (repo-internal), and it deliberately guards against mistaking near-output "action formatting" for genuine world content.

## 2. VLA and D: state of the art as of July 2026

### 2.1 Latent world models are now the winning form of D

The clearest 2026 signal is that the *cleanest* and *most performant* D is a learned latent world model, not a pixel reconstructor. **VLA-JEPA** attaches a JEPA-style latent world model with "leakage-free state prediction" — a target encoder produces latent targets from *future* frames while the student sees only the current observation, so future information is supervision-only and never an input — trained with a JEPA alignment loss rather than pixel reconstruction; a two-stage recipe (JEPA pretraining, then action-head fine-tuning) replaces prior 3+-stage latent-action pipelines. It reaches the highest LIBERO average (97.2%, vs OpenVLA-OFT 97.1% and pi0.5 96.9%), leads on 5/7 LIBERO-Plus perturbation dimensions, and tops SimplerEnv Google Robot, with robustness to camera motion and background changes [2][8]. This "leakage-free" design is significant for prisoma: it is a principled way to make D a *decision variable by construction*, which is a stronger guarantee than any extracted hidden state can offer.

All figures in this subsection are **paper-reported** benchmark numbers (traceable to the bracketed citations); treat them as such — benchmark- and protocol-dependent, not independently re-measured here. **LaWAM** predicts compact latent visual subgoals in the frozen DINOv3 latent space (via inverse-dynamics-inferred latent actions and a repurposed forward decoder) rather than reconstructing pixels or video, hitting 98.6% on LIBERO and 91.22% on RoboTwin at 187 ms per action-chunk and up to 24× lower wall-clock latency than pixel-space World-Action Models, using a 230M-parameter LaWM (~95% fewer world-modeling params) inside a 2.3B policy [11]. **AHEAD** augments a *frozen* 7B OpenVLA with a motion-aware latent world model that derives per-token velocity and acceleration from optical flow using only +4.9M parameters, targeting dynamic manipulation where objects move during execution; in simulation it reaches 79–97% across 20 dynamic scenarios (vs 31–58% for the best baseline), and on a physical xArm 7 it succeeds 29–30/30 on conveyor and rolling-ball and 19/30 on projectile catching where every baseline scores 0/30 [3]. **ALAM** learns algebraically-consistent latent transitions from action-free video (composition/reversal consistency: z_a^c ≈ z_a^b + z_b^c), yielding a locally additive latent space with 25–85× lower additivity/reversibility error than unstructured baselines, and transfer raises VLA success from 47.9% to 85.0% on MetaWorld MT50 and 94.1% to 98.1% on LIBERO [10]. **World-Value-Action (WAV)** does implicit planning in a structured latent trajectory space and *proves* that planning directly in action space suffers exponential decay in feasible-trajectory probability as horizon grows — a formal argument for why D (latent) beats A (raw action-space search) on long horizons [6].

The taxonomy work makes the trend explicit: WAMs partition into Render-and-Decode, Latent-Only, and Video-Generation-Free philosophies, decompose along predictive substrate / action coupling / architectural backbone / deployment regime, and the field is trending toward "generating less of the future while preserving what is needed for control" [5]. Robustness studies find WAMs generalize better than plain VLAs under visual and language perturbations — attributed to spatiotemporal priors from web-scale video pretraining — with LingBot-VA at 74.2% on RoboTwin 2.0-Plus and Cosmos-Policy at 82.2% on LIBERO-Plus, and hybrids intermediate, so *integration methodology matters* [9][79][80]. At the frontier, **NVIDIA Cosmos 3** unifies language, image, video, audio, and action in one mixture-of-transformers, subsuming VLMs, video generators, world simulators, and world-action models, ranked best open-source T2I/I2V and best policy by RoboArena at writing [4]; **GR00T N1.6** pairs a 32-layer diffusion-transformer action head with an internal Cosmos-2B VLM, unfreezes the top 4 VLM layers, and predicts state-relative action chunks, converging faster with smoother actions on real bimanual robots [7][72]. Predecessors GR00T N1.5/FLARE, V-JEPA 2, DreamVLA, WorldVLA, F1, and the latent-action lineage (LAPA, CLAM, UniVLA, Genie) frame the trajectory into this world-model-centric present [19][20][21][22][23][24][25][26][27].

### 2.2 Hidden states are probeable, value-like, and steerable

A second literature treats D as something to *read out of an existing policy* rather than to design in. **Frozen-VLA probing** shows lightweight linear probes on frozen features recover Monte-Carlo outcome/value targets from OpenVLA, Pi0.5, DINOv2, and CLIP (far better than progress/time-to-go/task-identity/proprioception baselines): under matched same-task/same-timestep comparisons, Pi0.5 probes reach ~92–94% pairwise ordering (vs ~50% shuffled), and as a test-time selector over sampled action prefixes the probe raises push-plate success from 26.7% greedy to 44.3% — a **+10.67pp over random (44.3% vs 33.7%) and +17.67pp over greedy** improvement [14] (the earlier "+16.67pp over random" figure is an illustrative-panel artifact, not the paper's formal table). A **mechanistic study of six VLA models** (80M–7B params, 394,000+ episodes, four benchmarks) finds the *visual pathway dominates* action generation across all architectures — injecting baseline activations into null-prompt episodes recovers near-identical behavior, and cross-task activation injection steers robots toward source-task positions (99.8% of X-VLA episodes align with the source trajectory) — while in multi-pathway models expert pathways encode motor programs and VLM pathways encode goal semantics [13]. **Sparse autoencoders** (TopK+AuxK) on pi0.5 residual streams yield ~79% interpretable features (sampled across pi0.5 and OpenVLA), with 97.38% memorized features in pi0.5-LIBERO vs 89.19% in pi0.5-DROID (diversity → more general features), and closed-loop steering causally alters behavior (a pre-grasp feature makes the robot hover; a transport feature makes it skip grasps) [15]. **Event-grounded SAEs** anchor feature analysis to behavioral keyframes rather than text contexts, yielding stronger causal effects on OpenVLA and transferring to continuous action chunks — but conclude SAEs are a "sparse yet imperfect intervention basis" whose usability varies by architecture and site, exposing safety limits [16][31]. Related emergent-representation and symbolic-state probing work (OpenVLA world representations, steering, cognitive-architecture integration) and the VLA-Trace diagnostic framework round out this read-out program [28][29][30][12]. The lesson for prisoma is twofold: D genuinely carries decision-relevant structure, *and* that structure is often entangled with the visual pathway and can be "output formatting" near the motor head — motivating the physics-probe-then-gate discipline.

### 2.3 Information-theoretic and PID analysis of multimodal internal state

A third, smaller literature applies PID directly. Applying PID to large VLMs decomposes decision-relevant information into redundant/unique/synergistic components (an "information spectrum"); profiling 26 LVLMs across four datasets reveals two task regimes (synergy-driven vs knowledge-driven), two family strategies (fusion-centric vs language-centric), and a three-phase layer-wise pattern with visual instruction tuning as the stage where fusion is learned [17]. In LLMs, a "synergistic core" concentrates in middle layers while early/late layers rely on redundancy; this emerges through training (absent at init), ablating synergistic components hurts far more than removing redundant ones, and RL fine-tuning of synergistic regions yields larger gains [18]. These are the direct scientific antecedents for prisoma's layerwise `D_hidden[k]` selection and for its unique/redundant/synergistic screens. Complementary multimodal-interaction methods (SynIB synergy bottleneck, modality-contribution disentangling, conditional utilization rate, sample-level interaction, and an information-theoretic capability/robustness bound for VLAs) map the surrounding design space [32][33][34][35][36].

### 2.4 PID estimator SOTA and its limits

prisoma's estimator core is pid-rs: shared-exclusions redundancy I^sx_cap (Makkeh–Gutknecht–Wibral), a continuous I^sx_cap estimator (Ehrlich et al.), and KSG mutual information (repo-internal) [39][40]. The 2026 estimator frontier matters for what NCP could ever clear. A closed-form **Gaussian multi-source PID** estimator gives explicit formulas (no iterative optimization), extends beyond two sources, validates finite-sample convergence with limited data, and ships open-source `gaussian-pid` [1][41][71]. But the theory is simultaneously getting *harder*: a comprehensive review catalogs the properties/measures of PID [37], and multiple 2025–2026 results establish **inconsistencies** in multivariate PID — subsystem inconsistency (the whole is less than the sum of parts), novel inconsistency results, and constructions that break naive extensions [38][65][66]. Related MI-estimation advances (high-dimensional MI, MIST supervised MI, task-relevant latent dimensionality, partial information *rate* decomposition) bear on the same small-n / high-d / autocorrelated regimes prisoma must survive [42][43][44][45]. prisoma's own Exp0 verdict on *clean* synthetic high-d controls (v=128, l=64, d=32, n=48) is **PIVOT/NO-GO** — the gate working as designed, not a bug (repo-internal). Any new D source inherits that fragile regime.

### 2.5 Failure diagnosis from internal state, and neuromorphic control

A fourth literature reads D to *predict failure*. **SAFE** does multitask failure detection for VLAs from internal features [46]; a wave of 2026 work extends this — perturbation-based uncertainty, FIPER runtime failure prediction, trajectory failure-signal discovery, flow-based UQ, uncertainty-at-critical-moments, an action-space probe, and failure-aware reconstruction [47][48][49][50][51][52][53][54]. This is precisely why the SAFE adapter is prisoma's critical-path producer: SAFE cleanly gives D = hidden states, A = actions, and success/failure labels (repo-internal).

Finally, the **neuromorphic/SNN** literature is where NCP would have to land as a *legitimate* D source. There now exist spiking world models with multicompartment neurons for model-based RL [55], SNNs for continuous control via end-to-end model-based learning [56], neuromodulation-based spiking controllers [57], analog spiking arm control [58], spike-driven decision transformers [59], spiking diffusion policy [60], HPC-scale embodied SNN simulation [61], and methods bridging discrete spikes to continuous control (Proxy Target, surrogate-gradient representation analysis) [62][63]. Crucially, the PID-in-neural-data literature shows synergy/redundancy is a *real, measurable* property of biological and spiking populations — parietal population codes, neuromorphic spike encoding, organotypic cultures where synergy is greater downstream of recurrent flow, time-varying synergy/redundancy dominance in human cortex, and electronic-oscillator networks [64][68][69][70][67] — with Gaussian PID bias-correction methods for high-dimensional neural data [71]. So the *idea* of PID on a spiking D is scientifically live; the question is whether *this* SNN, wired *this* way, qualifies.

## 3. Where prisoma sits

prisoma is not another VLA; it is a *gate-driven PID diagnostic* for VLA policies (repo-internal). Its distinctive commitments align cleanly with the four literatures above. (1) It makes D a **first-class, source-agnostic axis** and treats the layer choice for `D_hidden[k]` as an experimental variable selected by a layerwise physics-probe — the operationalization the LVLM/LLM PID papers imply but do not enforce [17][18]. (2) Its headline decomposition I(V,L;A) = Red + Unq(V) + Unq(L) + Syn, plus PID(V,D;A), plus the three-way CI(V,L,D;A)/I(V,L,D;A), turns the "information spectrum" idea into a per-source contract (repo-internal). (3) Its **PID(V,D;Flow)** target — where Flow is an intermediate 3D world-state bridge and Flow_gt is simulator ground truth — is a concrete world-model test in the WAM sense [5]: does D predict physically-valid 3D trajectory quantities, or is it merely action-formatting near the motor head? (4) It is *gate-first*: estimator + geometry gates must pass before any PID atom on real embeddings is interpreted, units are nats, and the Exp0 PIVOT/NO-GO verdicts on clean high-d controls are treated as the gate correctly refusing to certify signal below the noise floor (repo-internal). This is the same finite-sample humility the estimator literature now demands [1][37][38][42]. prisoma's falsifiable H1–H9 registry (H7 Flow-as-Bridge, H8 geometry gates, H9 attribution triangulation) preregisters kill criteria — dropping PID claims when simpler baselines suffice (repo-internal). In short, prisoma is a *diagnostic consumer* of the D that the world-model VLAs [2][3][10][11] and the probing/SAE literature [13][14][15][16] are producing.

## 4. How NCP fits

NCP (Neuro-Cybernetic Protocol; observer manifest pinned to immutable `v0.7.0`, wire 0.7) is an external Zenoh pub/sub protocol exposing **Engram**, a NEST spiking neural network, as a closed sensorimotor loop with three data planes: perception (SensorFrame, DROP QoS may drop messages), action (CommandFrame), and observation/neural-readback (ObservationFrame) (repo-internal). The `ncp-observer` crate is a **read-only passive tap** — it drives nothing; the Agent Bridge is the only control plane — and it maps NCP onto (V,L,D,A): V ← SensorFrame channels minus language/success; L ← a named SensorFrame channel (default "instruction"); since docset v10.6 absent-language ticks are excluded from the artifact and counted (`excluded_empty_l`), never zeroed; **D ← ObservationFrame record-port readouts = pre-motor neural state** (the "internal simulation" PID(V,D;A) targets); A ← CommandFrame channels, joined to V on `seq` (never arrival time, since DROP QoS makes time-pairing unsound). Every kept sample carries `l_source = "channel"` and exact `d_source = "seq"`; missing/seq-0/late D is dropped or excludes the tick rather than being paired by recency or patched after event emission (repo-internal).

The intended payoff is genuinely elegant and does *not* require Engram to be a great VLA. prisoma's PID screens quantify, per NCP sensor channel, the unique/redundant/synergistic information about A; that becomes a **design-time channel-prioritization policy** for the NCP perception codec under a low-bandwidth link (drop redundant, keep unique, bundle synergistic), and doubles as a **sim-vs-hardware (neuromorphic) fidelity metric**. PID here is design-time, never a per-tick runtime computation (repo-internal). This is a defensible engineering use even if the world-model story never holds — and it is consistent with the neuromorphic spike-encoding / information-maximization literature [68][64]. Critically, NCP is **OPTIONAL, exploratory-only, off the critical path, and excluded from the default cargo workspace**; the grandplan does not depend on Engram at all, and the pure-PID stack builds and gates green with no NCP/Engram/Zenoh dependency (repo-internal).

## 5. Is NCP sufficient?

### 5.1 Scientific validity — **INSUFFICIENT** (largely structural, not merely uncollected)

Is Engram a legitimate VLA analog, and is its pre-motor ObservationFrame readout a legitimate "D" comparable to a transformer VLA hidden state? The honest answer is no, today, and mostly *not by adding data*. Three disanalogies are structural (repo-internal). First, **Engram has no genuine language stream**: L is a named SensorFrame channel that is frequently absent and emitted as an empty/zeroed vector (`l_source = absent_zeroed`). A VLA is by definition vision-*language*-action; a source whose L axis is routinely fabricated cannot support the I(V,L;A) = Red+Unq(V)+Unq(L)+Syn decomposition that is prisoma's headline target. Second, the **"world model" label is unearned for a fixed pre-motor port**: prisoma's own §7.6.3 makes world-model-ness an *empirical, measurable* property (object speed/direction/contact decodability) and warns that high I(D;A) with low physics decodability "is measuring output formatting, not a world model." A pre-*motor* readout is exactly the near-output locus the physics-interpretability literature expects to be action-formatted rather than world-informative — physics decodability peaks at intermediate depth and degrades toward output layers [13], and the cleanest D comes from models whose predicted latents *are* the decision variables by construction [2][21], a guarantee an extracted SNN readout cannot offer. Third, the §7.6.3 hook-selection procedure (sweep layers, hook near the physics-probe peak, run the geometry gate) presupposes an ordered transformer layer stack; an SNN has no comparable stack to sweep, and the observer performs no probe-then-gate port selection — it simply concatenates whatever record-ports exist in BTreeMap order (repo-internal). These are architectural properties, not data-collection gaps.

What *is* scientifically sound is prisoma's **handling** of the weak analogy. The provenance markers stamp every degraded axis, the offline harness fails its geometry gate on any all-constant axis and offers `--require-axis-provenance-honest`, and the crate is workspace-excluded — so a degenerate SNN representation fails *closed* rather than silently emitting a fake PID atom (repo-internal). The project never asserts the analogy as science; it frames Engram as "another (V,L,D,A) source" under a source-agnostic contract, exploratory-only and below the M5 bar. So the integrity of the *program* holds; the *analogy* does not. Verdict on the literal lens claim: INSUFFICIENT.

### 5.2 Statistical / estimator adequacy — **INSUFFICIENT TODAY, FIXABLE in part**

No NCP data exists to test, so the question is answered structurally (repo-internal). As shaped today, `ncp-observer` artifacts fail the harness *before the estimator runs*: absent-L is a zero-length vector (validate_dataset bails on any empty axis; a zero-backfill instead trips the degenerate zero-variance guard → geometry "warn" → fail-closed); missing `metadata.split`/`episode_id`/`success` means no held-out split and none of the majority/1-NN/nearest-centroid/logreg baselines H1 must beat can be computed (strict modes fail closed); and `recency_fallback` D flips provenance to degraded (`--require-axis-provenance-honest` fails closed). This is the honesty machinery working, mirroring the Exp0 PIVOT/NO-GO posture — the claim is not "NCP passes" but "NCP would fail closed until the plumbing is honest," which is the correct default.

There is one genuine potential advantage: **low dimensionality**. Exp0's PIVOT/NO-GO is driven by high intrinsic dimension (joint ID ≈26–37 under pid-rs 0.4.0 bias-corrected, 28–42 under 0.3.0, vs the ID<20 gate) and MI collapse at d=64/256 — a curse of ambient+intrinsic dimension of VLA embeddings. A NEST closed loop (a handful of V channels, a few D ports, low-d A) is *plausibly* genuinely low-dimensional and could sit *under* the gates where v=128/l=64 synthetic and real d=4096 embeddings do not — the regime where KSG is most likely viable (repo-internal). But three estimator risks remain untested and could still fail the gates: (i) **spike-count D** readouts are integer-valued (`times.len()` as a scalar), and the continuous estimator runs Chebyshev KSG with k=3 and `tie_epsilon=0.0`, the exact tie pathology flagged as "especially vulnerable"; (ii) a **steady-state SNN** produces near-constant readouts → low pairwise CV (fails the >0.1 gate) or an outright degenerate axis; (iii) **recency_fallback** duplicates D across samples → near-zero pairwise distances, ties, and inflated-but-fake sample count. Two further traps: `MAX_INFLIGHT=4096` is an in-flight buffer bound, *not* a sample count (true n = session length minus DROP-QoS losses), and **temporal autocorrelation** silently shrinks effective n — consecutive closed-loop ticks are near-duplicates, and only the opt-in subsample bootstrap carries a block_size for it. Adopting a Gaussian-PID path [1][41] for approximately-Gaussian low-d readouts is a plausible sample-efficiency fix, but it is an *adoption recommendation*, not a current capability, since prisoma's continuous mode is KSG-based I^sx [39]. Verdict: INSUFFICIENT today; the plumbing is fixable, the estimator regime is genuinely uncertain.

### 5.3 Engineering completeness — **INSUFFICIENT TODAY, FIXABLE**

The three documented NCP gaps before it meets the contract bar are all engineering, and all repairable (repo-internal): (1) precise D `seq`-alignment — the **protocol** half is now resolved: wire 0.6 makes plane-published `ObservationFrame.seq >= 1` (echoing the driving `SensorFrame.seq`) *normative* and the observer enforces/counts it on ingress, so `recency_fallback` is no longer a protocol limitation but purely a producer-side one (the Engram publisher must actually stamp `obs.seq`, which prisoma cannot do from within this repo but the contract now mandates); (2) honest, non-zeroed L (a real language channel must be instrumented, not backfilled); (3) `metadata.split`/`episode_id`/`success` structure so held-out baselines and episode-disjoint splits exist. The passive-tap architecture, `seq`-join discipline, DROP-QoS-aware pairing, `MAX_INFLIGHT` bounding, and provenance stamping are already sound engineering. This lens is the most tractable — but note that gap (1) is partly out of prisoma's hands, and gap (2)'s only in-repo "fix" (a zero backfill or a text-hash proxy) is a *fabricated* language axis the geometry gate is designed to reject, so engineering completeness cannot manufacture scientific validity. Verdict: INSUFFICIENT today, fixable, with one external dependency.

### 5.4 Value vs the SAFE adapter — **SAFE dominates for the critical path**

SAFE (OpenVLA + pi0-FAST rollouts with success/failure outcomes) "cleanly gives D = hidden states, A = actions, labels," and the `safe_adapter` is *ready* — the single OPEN blocker is real-VLA data capture (repo-internal). SAFE is a genuine VLA with a real language stream, a real transformer layer stack for §7.6.3 hook selection, and labels for the H1 baselines — everything NCP structurally lacks. The failure-detection literature SAFE anchors is exactly where D-from-internal-state has demonstrated value [46][47][48][49][50][51][52][53]. NCP's distinctive value is *not* as a VLA substitute but as (a) a design-time codec-prioritization consumer of prisoma's PID screens and (b) a sim-vs-neuromorphic fidelity metric [68][64] — both real, both off the headline path. Verdict: for VLA studies and the VLDA contract, SAFE is sufficient-in-principle-and-ready; NCP is complementary and exploratory.

### 5.5 Integrated verdict

**NCP is not sufficient, today, for VLA studies or the (V,L,D,A)/VLDA PID contract.** The scientific lens is the binding constraint and is largely structural: no language stream, no probeable layer stack, a pre-motor D at the wrong locus for world-model content [13][2]. The statistical and engineering lenses are INSUFFICIENT-but-fixable, with two external/structural caveats (publisher-side `seq` stamping; the impossibility of synthesizing a real L). Against SAFE, NCP loses decisively as a *critical-path producer* but retains legitimate, narrower value as a design-time codec-optimization target and a fidelity metric. The correct posture — which the repo already enforces — is **exploratory, gated, non-headline**.

## 6. Recommendations

1. **Do not call Engram's pre-motor readout a "world model" or "internal simulation" in any result** until a §7.6.3-style physics/world-model decodability probe is run on the actual ports and passes. Downgrade in-repo wording to "pre-motor neural state (world-model status untested)."
2. **Treat Engram as a neural-dynamics source, not a VLA analog.** Given L is routinely `absent_zeroed`, restrict Engram screens to D-and-V atoms and drop the four-atom I(V,L;A) target for this source unless a *genuine* language channel is instrumented; do not accept a hash/zero proxy as L.
3. **Apply explicit port-selection before calling any port "D":** sweep record-ports with linear probes for task/physical variables and select by probe accuracy, mirroring §7.6.3 [17][18]; otherwise document D as "first-available-port," not a principled choice.
4. **Resolve the estimator regime for spike data:** separate analog-rate ports from spike-count ports, do not concatenate heterogeneous units into one continuous D, preregister continuous I^sx vs discrete I_min with the measure-identity/binning gate, report k-sensitivity, and evaluate a Gaussian-PID path for low-d approximately-Gaussian readouts [1][41][71]. Use block-subsampling for autocorrelated sessions [44].
5. **Keep the honesty scaffolding load-bearing:** never let an Engram atom into a comparison table without its `l_source`/`d_source`/geometry-gate status attached; keep `--require-axis-provenance-honest` mandatory on Engram artifacts.
6. **Prioritize the SAFE adapter as the real-VLA producer** and unblock the data capture; that, not NCP, closes M5.
7. **Realize NCP's actual payoff:** use prisoma PID screens as a *design-time* NCP codec channel-prioritization policy and as a sim-vs-neuromorphic fidelity metric [68][64] — never as a per-tick runtime computation.
